Local AI chat that respects your privacy.

OllamaTor is a user-friendly interface for Ollama, allowing you to chat with large language models (LLMs) directly on your computer. No data leaves your system.

Privacy

Your data stays local. No cloud, no tracking.

Performance

Leverage the full power of your computer for fast responses.

Customization

Choose from different models and adjust settings.

Offline Availability

Works even without an internet connection.

Open Source

The code is available on GitHub and can be reviewed by anyone.

User-Friendly

Easy installation and intuitive interface.
